Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly through Apple. Open your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Ryze.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

The free trial is available once per Apple ID. If not cancelled before the end of the trial period, the subscription automatically converts into a paid plan.

How do I restore purchases?

You can restore purchases inside the app using the “Restore Purchases” button on the subscription screen.

Does Ryze provide medical advice?

No. Ryze provides fitness and recovery guidance for informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting a new training program.
